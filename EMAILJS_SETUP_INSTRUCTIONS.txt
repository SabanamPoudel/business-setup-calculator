EmailJS Setup Instructions for Business Setup Cost Calculator
================================================================

You've successfully integrated EmailJS into your calculator! Follow these steps to complete the setup:

STEP 1: Get Your EmailJS Credentials
-------------------------------------
1. Go to https://www.emailjs.com/
2. Log in with your account (info@bizemirate.com)
3. Go to "Email Services" and note your Service ID
4. Go to "Email Templates" and create a new template (or note existing template ID)
5. Go to "Account" > "General" and copy your Public Key


STEP 2: Create an Email Template
---------------------------------
In your EmailJS dashboard, create a template with these variables:

Template Content Example:

STEP 3: Update the HTML File
-----------------------------
Open index.html and find these two lines:

Line ~1217:
    publicKey: "YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY", 
Replace "YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY" with your actual EmailJS Public Key

Line ~2621:
    emailjs.send('YOUR_SERVICE_ID', 'YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID', emailParams)
Replace:
- 'YOUR_SERVICE_ID' with your EmailJS Service ID
- 'YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID' with your EmailJS Template ID


STEP 4: Configure Email Service
--------------------------------
In EmailJS dashboard:
1. Add an email service (Gmail, Outlook, etc.) connected to info@bizemirate.com
2. Make sure the service is active
3. Test the connection


STEP 5: Test the Form
----------------------
1. Open the calculator in your browser
2. Fill out all 7 steps
3. Click Submit
4. Check info@bizemirate.com inbox for the email
5. Verify all data is received correctly


EXAMPLE VALUES (Replace with your actual values):
--------------------------------------------------
publicKey: "abcd1234efgh5678"
Service ID: "service_xyz123"
Template ID: "template_abc456"


TROUBLESHOOTING:
----------------
- If emails don't send, check browser console for errors
- Verify all three credentials are correct
- Check EmailJS dashboard for usage limits
- Make sure the email service is connected to info@bizemirate.com
- EmailJS free plan allows 200 emails/month


For more help, visit: https://www.emailjs.com/docs/
